# Arya Sharan - Portfolio

Welcome to my portfolio! I am an Embedded Systems & Hardware Designer, Electrical & Electronics Engineering Student at DSCE, and a Co-Founder at Lowwkey. 

## About Me

I specialize in bridging embedded firmware with structural semiconductor devices. My background spans hardware testing, PCB design, AI/ML (Computer Vision, SVMs), and digital marketing/entrepreneurship.

## Key Projects & Experience

- **Nunam Technologies (Intern)**: Researched and developed Portable Charging Stations utilizing repurposed second-life lithium-ion batteries.
- **Lowwkey (Co-Owner & Marketing Head)**: Co-founded and launched a niche, print-on-demand streetwear brand, managing business growth and digital marketing.
- **COURT_RECON**: A full-stack sports analytics dashboard for live basketball event logging and machine learning (SVM) matchup prediction.
- **CV Posture Analysis Bot**: An AI-based health assistant monitoring user posture using Computer Vision and MediaPipe.
- **Solar Powered Radio**: Prototyped a sustainable analog receiver with solar-charge circuits and battery integrations.
- **Robomanthan**: Engineered a Wi-Fi-controlled dynamic RC car with OTA updates and real-time gyroscope tracking.
